About college

Dhanalakshmi College of Engineering (DCE), an off-shoot of the Dhanalakshmi educational trust was started in the year 2001 at Tambaram, Chennai. DCE is the magnum opus of the eminent and prolific academician and former professor of Anna University Dr. V.P.Ramamurthi. DCE is the sister concern of the Indian Institute of Professional Education (IIPE, Saidapet, Chennai). The Institute offers six UG courses in engineering and technology and four PG courses MBA, MCA, ME (embedded systems, Computer science). All the courses have been approved by AICTE, New Delhi and four courses (ECE, EEE, CSE, IT) have been accredited by the NBA. DCE is affiliated to Anna University.
